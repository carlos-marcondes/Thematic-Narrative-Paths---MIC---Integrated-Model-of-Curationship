# Thematic-Narrative-Paths---MIC---Integrated-Model-of-Curationship
The research Thematic Narrative Paths - MIC - Integrated Model of Curationship aims at devepoling Thematic Narrative Paths, a cross-cutting theme across different digital collections. 
It is formed by digital objects from collections of different Brazilian heritage institutions. Such TNP are modeled according to MIC - Integrated Model of Curationship, and serialized 
in RDF, TriG and named graphs.
